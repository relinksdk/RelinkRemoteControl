Remote Relink Control purpose

We needed to be able to change the sound on an android remotely, to send any kind of messages easily without reinventing the wheel. We decided to use what is know by everybody already, Intent. Replace Intent by RemoteIntent, add the receiver ID and that's it :)

Intent intent = RemoteIntent(<remote_id>, "SET_SOUND"); intent.putExtra("level", 80); startActivity(intent);

That's it. On the remote device, just catch the intent and do what you want with.
